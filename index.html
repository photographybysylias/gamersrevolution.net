How to join:

1. Open a Minecraft client running 1.8.9 or newer.
2. Click on Multiplayer, then click on Direct Connect (you can also add a server)
3. Input mc.hypixel.net and click connect.
4. Run the command /lobby housing, once you are in the Housing lobby, run /visit qTxddy
5. Click on the housing you would like to join!
